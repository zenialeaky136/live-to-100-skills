# Reminder Presets

Adapt these defaults to the user's wake/sleep schedule and constraints.

## 1) Hydration

- Type: Drink water
- Frequency: Every 2 hours during wake window
- Message: "喝水 250-350 ml，完成后打卡。"
- Completion rule: 6-10 check-ins/day

## 2) Standing / mobility

- Type: Stand and move
- Frequency: Every 50-60 minutes during work/study blocks
- Message: "起身活动 3-5 分钟，做一次肩颈和髋部拉伸。"
- Completion rule: >= 8 mobility breaks/day

## 3) Sleep routine

- Type: Wind-down
- Timing: 60 minutes before target bedtime
- Message: "进入睡前流程：停咖啡因、关强光、放下屏幕、准备入睡。"
- Completion rule: 5+ nights/week on schedule

## 4) Workout

- Type: Planned training
- Timing: User-defined (usually fixed 3-5 days/week)
- Message: "开始今日训练：先热身 8-10 分钟，再执行主训练。"
- Completion rule: Hit weekly session target

## 5) Supplements

- Type: Supplement reminder
- Timing: With meals or fixed slot per supplement requirements
- Message: "按既定剂量补充，不超量；若不适或与药物冲突，先停用并咨询医生。"
- Completion rule: Record taken/skipped + reason

## Adjustment rules

- If adherence < 70% in a week, reduce frequency by 20% before raising targets.
- If sleep debt > 2 hours for 3+ days, lower exercise intensity for 48 hours.
- If pain/fatigue flags persist > 7 days, suggest professional follow-up.
